Tallahassee Marathon and Half Marathon
Sunday, February 7th, 2016, either one shift from 8am – 2pm, or two 3-hour shifts from 8-11am and then 11am-2pm
Volunteers needed to:
  • Direct runners on the course.
  • Be at the finish line to help runners as they complete the race, including giving them water, medals, and handing out food.
  • Help keep cars out of the path of runners.
  • Age requirement: All ages, the youngest kids can hand out medals or just cheer.
  • 30 to 40 volunteers needed
All jobs are easily explained and do not require prior knowledge or special expertise, but do require people to pay attention.
This year will mark the 42nd edition of the Tallahassee Marathon (www.tallahasseemarathon.com), which is run 100% by volunteers and all profits are given to local charities.
Signup at Tallahassee Marathon Volunteer
Contact: Eric Godin, co-Director, Tallahassee Marathon at eegodin@gmail.com or call 610-291-9345 with any questions.
