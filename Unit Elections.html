Semialachee Lodge 239 is proud to announce that our 2016 unit election period is now open. To schedule your unit election please use the email provided in the letter sent out to scoutmasters, or semialacheelodge@gmail.com IMPORTANT! If you have booked your election on the old scheduling system that used to appear on this page (prior to march 21st 2016), we do not have your information (due to a technical problem) and you will have to send an email to schedule your election!
